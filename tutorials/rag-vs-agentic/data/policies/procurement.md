# Procurement Policy

## Approval Thresholds
Purchases under $200 can be manager-approved.
Purchases from $200 to $999 require manager plus finance approval.
Purchases of $1,000 or more require manager, finance, and director approval.

## Preferred Vendors
Employees must use preferred vendors when available.
If a preferred vendor is unavailable, document 2 comparable quotes.

## Software and Services
Any software subscription that stores company or customer data requires security review before purchase regardless of cost.

# Remote Work Policy

## Eligibility
Employees in roles marked remote-eligible may work from home up to 3 days per week with manager approval.

## Home Office Expenses
A one-time home office setup stipend of $300 is available for new remote employees in their first 60 days.
Additional equipment purchases above $300 must follow Procurement Policy approval thresholds.

## Internet and Utilities
Monthly internet reimbursement is capped at $50 for remote-eligible employees.
Utility reimbursements are not covered unless required under local law.

## Travel to Office
Commutes to an assigned office are not reimbursable.
Travel to a different office for a required meeting may be reimbursable if approved in advance.

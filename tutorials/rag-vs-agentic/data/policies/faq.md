# Policy FAQ

## Common Edge Cases
If a meal exceeds the daily cap due to a hosted client event, the overage may be reimbursed with manager pre-approval and event details.
If a trip spans multiple cities in one day, use the destination city where dinner occurs for that day meal multiplier.

## Combined Policy Clarifications
A remote employee visiting HQ for a routine commute cannot expense commuting costs.
A remote employee traveling to a non-assigned office for a planned business meeting can expense travel if pre-approved.

## Documentation Rules
Receipt rules follow Expense Policy thresholds.
Per diem is claimed separately and should not be merged into meal line items.

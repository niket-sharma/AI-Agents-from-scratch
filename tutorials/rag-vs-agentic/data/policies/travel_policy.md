# Travel Policy

## Purpose
This policy defines reimbursable travel limits, per diem components, and city-based multipliers.

## City Multipliers for Meals
Meal multiplier by city is:
- New York City (NYC): 1.40
- San Francisco (SF): 1.25
- Seattle: 1.15
- All other cities: 1.00

The effective daily meal cap is calculated as base meal allowance multiplied by city multiplier.

## Lodging Caps
Maximum reimbursable nightly lodging rates before taxes and fees:
- NYC: $300 per night
- SF: $260 per night
- Seattle: $220 per night
- All other domestic cities: $180 per night

## Per Diem
Incidental per diem is $20 per travel day and is separate from meal allowance.
Per diem does not require receipts.
